About


Trevor Hawkins
Exploration Manager
Trevor is a senior geologist at Hawkeye Mineral Exploration LLC.

Korbon McCall
Sr. Exploration Geologist
Korbon is an Idaho native with a B.Sc. in Geology from University of Idaho. He developed his passion for exploration during a stint at the Idaho Geological Survey. His past work includes supervisory roles in soil sampling, claim staking, core drilling/logging programs, and QA/QC procedures. Korbon has completed these projects across many states including Idaho, Nevada, Utah, Colorado, Wyoming, and Montana. His military service taught him to be pragmatic and practical, and has lent to the determination to do what it takes to get the job done.

Shawn Gahagan
Exploration Geologist
Shawn is a New York native currently working towards his M.Sc. in Igneous Petrology at Washington State University, where he studies the origin of Snake River-type rhyolites and field applications of portable XRF technology. Shawn acquired his experience in the industry by working on RC-drilling, logging, and soil sampling programs in Nevada, and a core drilling/logging program in central Idaho. During his studies and work, Shawn has picked up valuable experience with Microsoft Suite, ArcGIS, and acQuire; along with a passion for petrology, litho-geochemistry, and QA/QC.

Aryan Parthor
Exploration Geologist
Aryan is from New York, and has a B.Sc. in Geology from Boise State University. His mineral exploration career began on a gold mine in central Idaho. During his career Aryan has managed databases, logged core, field mapped, and modeled with ArcGIS. Through his time working in politics, Aryan became proficient in database software and languages (including SQL), Microsoft Office, and programming languages like MatLab and Python. He excels in communication with groups of differentiating ideas and values to find solutions and develop short and long term strategies. Aryan speaks Farsi (Persian), and is pursuing French.
